Who we are : eBanking

Carnegie Financial Services (CFS) offers ebank services, known as eBanking. Our satisfied customers range from major corporations, such as Moffet Foods and Big Red Tractors, to wealthy individuals and to large numbers of professionals, educators, and students of all income ranges.


Our founder :

The late Dr. Harry Q. Bovik had the vision to make all banking transactions electronic way back in the 1980’s, before the web came into existence. His first business, Long Range Transactions, was acquired by Manufacturer’s Hanover in 1990, and he dedicated the proceeds to making CFS a reality. After many difficult years, Dr. Bovik made eBanking really work. Now CFS is proud to be the premier pure eBank!



  What we offer :

e-Loans

Business and personal loans of all types are available for qualified individuals and enterprises – and it’s easy, apply on line, and in many cases approval will be virtually instantaneous. We offer low rates!

e-Checking

CFS makes it easy. Just fill in the eApplication form send us an initial deposit – any amount over $100, and volia!, you are the proud owner of a CFS checking account. You can send eChecks, send and receive PayPal, wire transfers, and yes, even paper checks. The best part is that CFS eChecking is totally free!

eCDs

Yes, we also offer very attractive Certificate of Deposits, from 6 months to 5 years at interest rates you wouldn’t believe! We can do this because we don’t have to pay for fancy bank buildings, or large numbers of tellers, or branch managers. So we pass on a big part of the savings to you as higher interest rates. CFS is your kind of bank!

e-Mortgages

We offer the best rate on regular and jumbo mortgages to qualified individuals on their residences. Fill in our application, credit report, and permission for inspection, and you are on your way!

e-Savings

CFS also make is very easy. Fill in the eApplication, send a deposit, and set up a transfer between eChecking and eSavings and you are in business! We offer attractive interest rates, and the convenience of banking from anywhere at anytime it suits your needs.
