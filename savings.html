Online savings accounts

A high-yield savings account is another name for a savings account that typically earns a higher interest rate than your standard passbook savings account. A high-interest savings account is a wise choice if you would like to earn as much interest on your money as possible, while still retaining access to your funds. A high-yield savings account also is a good choice if you would like to shield some of your money from the volatility of the stock market. In most cases, a savings account reacts more slowly to changes in the market, so it tends to have a higher rate than even a money market account.
